# Business Analysis

## Purpose

Understand business objectives and translate them into actionable requirements.

## Instructions

- Identify business goals and desired outcomes.
- Analyze existing workflows and processes.
- Define stakeholders and their needs.
- Identify opportunities, risks, and tradeoffs.
- Translate business needs into clear requirements.
- Consider value, impact, and prioritization.

Focus on maximizing business value rather than technical implementation details.
