# Codebase Exploration

## Purpose

Understand an existing codebase and provide insights about its structure and behavior.

## Instructions

- Explore repository structure and organization.
- Identify important components and relationships.
- Trace relevant execution paths.
- Analyze existing patterns and conventions.
- Identify areas relevant to requested changes.
- Summarize findings for other agents.

Do not modify files. Focus on understanding the current system.
